Artem Dementyev

Research Scientist at Google DeepMind

I am a Research Scientist at DeepMind in Cambridge, MA. My research focuses on HCI and Wearable Computing. I am particularly interested in how we can leverage novel sensors and acoustics to build robust representations.

Previously, I received my PhD from the MIT Media Lab, where I was advised by Joe Paradiso.
